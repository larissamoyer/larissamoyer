MISSION STATEMENT

Jane Doe is an anonymous political group that was formed to bring attention to injustices through political art. Jane Doe wants to raise awareness so change can be made. The identity of the member(s) is concealed in order to bring attention only to the issues and not to the member(s). The member(s) do not want to take away from the importance of the issues at hand.
